Defense Gaps report

The defense gaps displayed in these plots are probably the result of a system discipline issue, and the lack of habit of playing together. Also, given the physical difference at the net, the block cannot probably cover the right zone efficiently. To fix that we can try to respect our defense positions, and adjust to the hitting zones, especially when the other team is hitting through the middle. A lot of cross court hitting.
